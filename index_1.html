Professional Summary

Extension Officer & Digital Transformer

Abu Md. Moniruzzaman is an Additional Deputy Director (Horticulture) at the Department of Agricultural Extension (DAE), Government of Bangladesh, posted at Kurigram. A member of the 31st BCS (Agriculture) Cadre, he brings a distinctive blend of corporate leadership, entrepreneurial acumen, and field-level extension expertise.

His professional journey spans from corporate sales management (Samsung, Symphony) and agro-entrepreneurship (Nagar Krishak Limited) to civil service — equipping him with rare competencies in resource optimization, modern management, and technology-driven agricultural transformation. He holds an MBA from North South University and a B.Sc. Agriculture from HSTU.

With over 8 years of field experience across geographically diverse settings — haor wetlands of Sunamganj, hill tracts of Rangamati, and plains of Rangpur — he has consistently championed sustainable agriculture, digital extension services, and evidence-based crop management for Bangladesh's smallholder farmers.

"Transforming Bangladesh's agricultural extension from paper-based to AI-powered — bringing science, data, and connectivity directly into the hands of smallholder farmers, while building a Smart Bangladesh through technology, transparency, and field-level dedication."

— Abu Md. Moniruzzaman, Vision Statement
